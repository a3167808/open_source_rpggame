## Card Management

## Combat
Player combat is initialised by player encounters on the same territory square. 

The user selects a card from their deck to play. The effectiveness of the card is determined by the memory flash (see section below). Card actions are then executed simultaneously. Combat is resolved after:
- 3 rounds of combat, or
- One party has no health remaining

# Memory Flash (Combat)
The memory flash is a mini-actionable which determines the effectiveness of a card played in combat. The player is given a random series of numbers for a given time limit, whereby the number of digits and time is determined by a difficulty level variable. The player must then input as many digits as possible in the correct sequence. The more digits the player inputs correctly, the more effective the card played in combat will be.
